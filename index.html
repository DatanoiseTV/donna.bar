The Venue

DONNA is more than just a bar – it's a cultural hub where Berlin's creative community converges. Located in the vibrant Graefekiez neighborhood, we've become the go-to spot for musicians, artists, and anyone who appreciates authentic live performances.

Our intimate space creates the perfect atmosphere for those legendary jam sessions you've heard about. Whether you're here for the music, the drinks, or the company, DONNA offers an experience that captures the essence of Berlin's underground culture.
